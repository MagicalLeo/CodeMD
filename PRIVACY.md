# Privacy Policy for CodeMD

**Last updated: December 13, 2024**

## Overview

CodeMD is a Markdown reader application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**CodeMD does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- All files are processed locally on your device
- No user accounts or registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No data is sent to external servers (except for rendering Mermaid diagrams via CDN)

## File Access

CodeMD only accesses files that you explicitly choose to open. The app does not scan or access any other files on your device.

## Third-Party Services

- **Mermaid.js CDN**: Used to render diagrams. No personal data is transmitted.
- **Google AdMob**: May display ads. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information on how Google handles data.

## Children's Privacy

CodeMD does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
